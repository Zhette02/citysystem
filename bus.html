Waiting for the bus

Language barriers are something I think about alot. On this bus ride there were 4 different languages being spoken all at once. Having some experience navigating public transportation systems that are not in my native language, I know it can be a challenge. I wonder how people do it in NYC. This seems like one of the strangest systems that I have encountered. NYCs public transit system feels like its falling apart at all times. Like its alive and the MTA are caretakers. During my first year living here I got lost constantly. I still dont know how we all get where we need to go.
